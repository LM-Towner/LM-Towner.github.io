About Me

As a career changer, what I lack in traditional training, I make up in ambition. Every day I strive to become a better person. I am not a stranger to hard work. I spent four years studying Chemistry to obtain my Bachelors, which is no small accomplishment. Even after this major milestone, I wanted a change, even though my ambition was to become a pharmacist. I soon realized that I had fallen out of love with it. What's the point of doing something for money if you don't enjoy it?

Currently, I am striving to become a Software Developer professionally. I've taken additional University classes, as well as learned on the job. My interests lie within the backend portion of the development cycle. Database Management and configuring servers is what interests me the most. I've also taken a liking to mobile development and would love to work on projects revolving the development on these applications.

If you would like to know more about me, check out my blog where I dug deeper into my adversaries, and my triumphs.

Check out my current resume as well if you would like to work together on a project.
